##### Required Documents for Opening a BO Account

This guide will help you identify the specific documents needed to open various types of BO accounts in the Bangladesh capital market.

##### 1. For Individual / Joint / Link BO Accounts

- Photographs: 2 copies of passport-size photos for each applicant and the nominee(s).
- Identification: Photocopy of National ID (NID) card or Passport.
- Bank Details: A photocopy of a leaf from an MICR checkbook OR a Bank Certificate mentioning the 9-digit routing number and 13-digit bank account number.
- TIN Certificate: Photocopy of the Taxpayer's Identification Number (TIN) certificate (where applicable).

##### 2. For Non-Resident Bangladeshi (NRB) BO Accounts

- Photographs: 2 copies of passport-size photos for each applicant and the nominee(s).
- Identification: Attested copies of NID / Passport / Social Security Card / Residence Permit, etc.
- FC Bank Account: Details and information regarding your Foreign Currency (FC) bank account.
- Nominee ID: Photocopy of the Nominee’s National ID card.
- Power of Attorney (POA): A POA form authorizing a representative to operate the account must be filled out and notarized on a 300 Taka non-judicial stamp.

##### 3. For Company / Institutional BO Accounts

- Constitutional Docs: Memorandum and Articles of Association.
- Form-XII: A copy of Form-XII collected from the RJSC (Registrar of Joint Stock Companies and Firms).
- Board Resolution: A photocopy of the company’s Board Resolution authorizing the opening of the BO account.
- Power of Attorney (POA): 1 passport-size photo of the POA (where applicable).
- Contact Person: 3 passport-size photos and photocopies of both sides of the National ID (NID) of the designated contact person.
- Bank Details: Bank Certificate mentioning the 9-digit routing number and 13-digit account number, along with a photocopy of an MICR checkbook leaf.
- Tax & License: Photocopy of the TIN Certificate and Trade License.
- Seal: Official Company Seal.

##### Processing Time

Typically, a BO account is opened within two working days after the properly filled form and documents are submitted. Once the account is active, you can begin your investment journey by depositing cash or a check.
